# PrimeHub Systems — User Manual

PrimeHub Systems is an **all-in-one operations platform** that combines IT asset management, employee attendance tracking, form request workflows, and coaching management into a single unified system.

Designed for organizations of all sizes, it gives every role — from agents and team leads to HR, IT, and administrators — the tools they need to manage day-to-day operations efficiently. Track hardware inventory across sites and campaigns, monitor biometric attendance with violation point systems, handle leave and IT support requests through automated approval workflows, and oversee coaching sessions — all within one integrated application.

Built for simplicity without sacrificing depth, PrimeHub Systems replaces scattered spreadsheets and manual processes with a centralized, role-aware hub that keeps your operations running smoothly.

| Role | What You Can Do |
|------|----------------|
| **Agent** | Log attendance, submit leave and IT requests, track your own coaching sessions, manage break timers |
| **Team Lead** | View team attendance and schedules, approve leave and IT requests, conduct coaching sessions |

---

## Modules at a Glance

- **Dashboard** — Your personalized landing page. What you see depends on your role: attendance summaries, coaching follow-ups, pending approvals, and more.
- **Attendance** — Manage biometric records, employee schedules, attendance points, anomaly detection, and data uploads. *The most feature-rich module in the system.*
- **Break Timer** — Start, pause, and end break sessions with a live countdown. Supervisors can monitor all agents, enforce policies, and generate reports.
- **Coaching** — Create and track one-on-one coaching sessions between team leads and agents. Includes escalation checks, draft support, and compliance dashboards.
- **Form Requests** — Submit and approve leave applications, IT support tickets, and medication requests — each with its own multi-step workflow.
- **Computer** — Full PC specification tracking (processor, RAM, disk, monitor type, ports), processor spec catalog, PC maintenance records, and hardware issue tracking.
- **Station** — Workstation management with site and campaign assignments, QR code generation, PC transfers, and monitor configuration.
- **Account** — User profiles, role assignment, activity logs, two-factor authentication, and account settings.

## Getting Started

1. **Log In** — Open PrimeHub Systems in your browser. Enter your company email and password.
2. **Dashboard** — After login, you arrive at your **Dashboard**. The **left sidebar** lists every module you have access to. Click any item to open it.
3. **Notifications** — The **bell icon** in the top bar shows real-time alerts. Click it to view or dismiss notifications.
4. **Profile & Settings** — Click your **avatar** in the top-right corner to update your password, appearance, preferences, or enable two-factor authentication.

*If you cannot log in, contact your administrator. New accounts require admin approval before first access.*
